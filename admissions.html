Frequently Asked Questions

What is the application deadline?

Applications for Term 1 close on October 31 each year. Late applications may be considered depending on availability.

Do you offer financial aid or scholarships?

We offer limited bursaries for deserving families based on need and academic performance. Please contact our office for details.

Can I tour the school before applying?

Absolutely! We host open days each term. Book a tour via email or call our office to arrange a visit.
