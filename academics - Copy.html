Our Academic Programmes

Igniting curiosity, building skills, and nurturing young minds for lifelong learning.

Core Curriculum

Mathematics

Developing strong numeracy, problem-solving, and logical thinking skills through hands-on learning.

  • Number sense & arithmetic
  • Geometry & measurement
  • Data handling & probability

Languages

Building literacy, communication, and cultural appreciation in English and home languages.

  • Reading, writing & comprehension
  • Oral communication skills
  • Creative expression

Natural Sciences

Exploring the world through observation, experimentation, and inquiry-based learning.

  • Life & environmental sciences
  • Physical sciences
  • Scientific method

Social Sciences

Fostering knowledge of history, geography, culture, and responsible citizenship.

  • Local & world history
  • Geography & sustainability
  • Civics & community awareness

Enrichment Programmes

Art & Creativity Club

Encouraging imaginative expression through painting, drawing, and crafts.

Sports & Physical Education

Promoting teamwork, fitness, and motor skills with a range of athletic activities.

Science & Nature Club

Experiments, outdoor learning, and critical thinking in a fun environment.

Music & Drama

Fostering confidence and creativity through performance and ensemble work.

Our Teaching Approach

At Rustenburg Noord Primary, we believe in a balanced, child-centered approach that combines:

  • Active learning through discussion, exploration, and real-world connections
  • Individualized support to help each learner reach their potential
  • Collaborative work to build social skills and peer learning
  • Assessment for learning: ongoing feedback rather than just exams
